Learn C

C is a timeless programming language revered for its speed, simplicity, and influence on modern computing. Developed in the 1970s by Dennis Ritchie at Bell Labs, C has since become the bedrock of software engineering. Its efficiency and portability make it ideal for system programming, operating systems, and embedded systems. C serves as the foundation for languages like C++, Java, and C#, showcasing its enduring impact. Renowned for its direct hardware access and low-level manipulation, C empowers developers to optimize performance. Whether crafting robust operating systems or high-performance applications, C's enduring legacy solidifies its place in the programming pantheon
